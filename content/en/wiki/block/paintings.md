## Gallifrey Falls Painting

**“That’s where you got it wrong, it’s all in one title: Gallifrey Falls No More.” - The Curator**

A bigger on the inside painting, inspired by [the one scene](https://tardis.wiki/wiki/Gallifrey_Falls_No_More) in the special episode "[The Day of the Doctor](https://tardis.wiki/wiki/The_Day_of_the_Doctor_(TV_story))".

## Trenzalore Painting

**"When the TARDIS is dying, sometimes the dimension dams start breaking down. They used to call it a size leak. All the bigger on the inside starts leaking to the outside and it grows." - The Eleventh Doctor.**

A bigger on the inside painting, inspired by the sights of the planet [Trenzalore](https://tardis.wiki/wiki/Trenzalore) seen in the special "[The Name of the Doctor](https://tardis.wiki/wiki/The_Name_of_the_Doctor_(TV_story))".
