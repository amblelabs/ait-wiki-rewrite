By placing it down in a rift chunk, The artron collector will collect artron energy from the chunk until there is none left. You can check how much artron is in a chunk by using your sonic screwdriver in scanning mode in a rift chunk. You can `right click` the artron collector with an {{< term "Artron Collecto Unit" >}} to store the artron energy or put it in a TARDIS.
